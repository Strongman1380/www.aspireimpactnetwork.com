How To Practice Each Meditation

Learn the step-by-step techniques for each meditation practice

üåÖ Morning Intention Setting

Set a positive direction for your day and align with your recovery values.

  1. 1Find a quiet space and sit comfortably with your spine straight
  2. 2Close your eyes and take 3 deep breaths to center yourself
  3. 3Reflect on your recovery journey and feel gratitude for your progress
  4. 4Ask yourself: "How do I want to show up today?"
  5. 5Choose one positive intention (e.g., "I will be kind to myself")
  6. 6Visualize yourself living this intention throughout your day
  7. 7End with gratitude and slowly open your eyes

üöÄ Stress Relief Meditation

Release tension and find calm during challenging moments.

  1. 1Sit or lie down in a comfortable position
  2. 2Close your eyes and notice where you feel tension in your body
  3. 3Begin breathing slowly: 4 counts in, 4 counts hold, 6 counts out
  4. 4With each exhale, imagine releasing stress and tension
  5. 5Scan your body from head to toe, consciously relaxing each part
  6. 6If your mind wanders, gently return to your breath
  7. 7Rest in this peaceful state for the remaining time

üõ°Ô∏è Craving Management

Navigate urges and cravings with mindfulness and self-compassion.

  1. 1As soon as you notice a craving, pause and sit down
  2. 2Acknowledge the craving without judgment: "I notice I'm having a craving"
  3. 3Observe where you feel it in your body (tension, heat, restlessness)
  4. 4Breathe into that area with kindness and compassion
  5. 5Remind yourself: "This feeling is temporary and will pass"
  6. 6Visualize the craving as a wave that rises and falls
  7. 7Connect with your deeper "why" for recovery
  8. 8Stay present until the intensity decreases

üåô Evening Gratitude

End your day with appreciation and peaceful reflection.

  1. 1Find a comfortable position in a quiet space
  2. 2Close your eyes and take several deep, calming breaths
  3. 3Reflect on your day with kindness, not judgment
  4. 4Identify 3 things you're grateful for today (big or small)
  5. 5Feel the warmth of gratitude in your heart as you think of each one
  6. 6Express gratitude for your body and your commitment to recovery
  7. 7Set a peaceful intention for restful sleep
